The Store X is a platform for ideas and culture.

Our program encompasses fashion, art, design and beyond, engaging a global community of innovators and curious creatives in our spaces at 180 The Strand in London, Soho House Berlin and Soho Farmhouse in Oxfordshire. Our ambition is to present a new kind of experiential space that transcends our expectations of traditional retail, beyond shopping.
